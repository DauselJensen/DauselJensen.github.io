How I stay organised

Clickup as planning tool

To keep track of all the tasks, such as what programming tasks need to be done, what needs to be animated, and also to remember future plans. Everything is organised in to-do lists in ClickUp, furthermore that is also where I keep track of all my logs. This site is used every sunday, to plan the upcoming week, and also used throughout the weekdays, to keep as a checklist for the goals of the week.

Microsoft To Do as a checklist

Click up is used in the big picture, to keep track of all my hobby-relevant projects. To keep track of small everyday-tasks I use Microsoft ToDo which I have been doing for many years. Clickup is something I started using in beginning of 2024.
